# About the project

This project explores urban collectives and how they organize, locate and describe themselves.

## Context

Urban collectives often emerge where people want to shape spaces, structures or forms of living together. They can be informal, formalized, political, practical, long-term or temporary.

## Why this tool?

The tool is meant to help collectives make their own way of working visible. It is not about assigning a fixed category, but about making differences and priorities easier to understand.

## How does the test work?

The test asks several questions about organization, temporality, identity and spatial anchoring. At the end, these answers create a graphic profile of your collective. You can also prioritize goals, which changes the color gradient of the graphic.

## What happens to the results?

The results can be used to make different collectives more comparable. In the long term, they could appear on the homepage if consent has been given.
